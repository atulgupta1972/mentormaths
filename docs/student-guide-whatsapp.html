Step 1

Open your Dashboard

  1. Go to mentormaths.in and log in.
  2. You see your Dashboard for the year.
  3. Left side — your school exams & prep progress.
  4. Right side — practice sheets to do (Start / Continue).
  5. Top bar shows counts: exams · to do · done.
Screen 2 — Add / edit exam (same page, form opens below)
mentormaths.in/dashboard
Dashboard → tap Add / edit exams

Add exam plan

Select topics ↗
Step 2

Enter your school exam

  1. Tap Add / edit exams (purple button, left side).
  2. Choose exam date and type (unit test / half yearly / final).
  3. Enter a title e.g. “Unit test 1”.
  4. Tick ☑ each chapter in your school test.
  5. Optional: tap Select topics to pick only some topics inside a chapter.
  6. Tap Save exam plan.

Your exam card appears on the left with days left & prep sets.

Step 3

Start your practice sheet

  1. On Dashboard, look at Practice & tests · To do (right, yellow area).
  2. Find your set code — e.g. S711, S712.
  3. Tap Start (new sheet) or Continue (already started).
  4. Teacher assigns sheets linked to your exam chapters.

Solve sums on your printed worksheet first, then record answers online.

Step 4

Attempt & submit

  1. Work on your worksheet on paper (or PDF).
  2. On screen, for each question tap your choice: A B C D.
  3. Match Q1, Q2, Q3… on paper to rows on screen.
  4. Answer all questions, then tap Submit.
  5. You see your score (e.g. 6/7) immediately.

Late submit is OK but marked as delayed. Completed sets show on Dashboard.
