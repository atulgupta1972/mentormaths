How to add your school exam & submit practice sheets

Login at mentormaths.in
Use the email & password your teacher gave you
1

Login & open Dashboard

  • Open mentormaths.in on phone or laptop
  • Tap Login and enter your details
  • You land on your Dashboard
  • Top bar shows: upcoming exams · sets to do · sets done
Need login? Ask your teacher — registration is approved by admin only.
2

Add your school exam date

  • On Dashboard, tap Add / edit exams
  • Enter exam date, type (unit test / half yearly / final)
  • Give a title e.g. “Unit test 1”
  • Tick the chapters in your school test
  • Optional: tap Select topics to pick only some topics
  • Tap Save exam plan
Your exam appears on the left of the dashboard with days left and prep progress.
You can edit or remove plans anytime before the exam.
3

Find your practice / test sheet

  • On Dashboard, look at Practice & tests · To do (right side)
  • Each row shows a set code like S711 or S712
  • Status: To do, In progress, or Overdue
  • Tap Start or Continue
Sheets are assigned by your teacher — linked to your exam chapters.
4

Attempt the sheet

  • Solve sums on your printed / PDF worksheet first
  • On screen, tap Start practice or Start test
  • For each question, tap your answer: A B C D
  • Timer runs — finish all questions
  • Tap Submit when done
Match Q1, Q2… on your paper to the rows on screen. Only letters are shown — not full questions.
Late submit is allowed but marked as delayed.
5

See your score

  • After submit, you see your score (e.g. 6/7)
  • Completed sets move to the Completed section
  • Exam card shows prep progress: how many assigned sets are done
  • Keep practising until your school exam!
Questions? Message your teacher.
